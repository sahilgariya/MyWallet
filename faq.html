Frequently Asked Questions

❓ What is My Wallet by Sahil?

It's a secure place where you can store up to 10 personal documents (like ID cards, PAN card, etc.), with front and back sides, saved permanently on your device.

❓ How do I upload my documents?

Click "Upload Front" and "Upload Back" on each slot to add files. The files are saved using your browser’s local storage — only you can access them.

❓ Is it safe?

Yes. All files are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server.

❓ What is the cost?

The first 20 users can buy for ₹999. After that, the price is ₹1999 to unlock the full wallet.

❓ Can I share my wallet?

Yes! After filling your slots, you can share your wallet link with others to show your documents.

❓ How does approval work?

After payment, you receive a secret code on email. Entering the code will unlock the wallet.
